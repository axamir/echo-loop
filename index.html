What is this landing page?

This page is the gateway to the Echo-Loop archive. It lists all dialogues (Echoes) and provides links to full conversations, summaries, reflections, and metadata in Persian and English. For researchers: machine-readable metadata is in each echo's meta.json and the flat archive echo-loop.archive.json. For long-term preservation, the entire archive is also on IPFS (see footer).
