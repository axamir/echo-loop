📖 What is Echo-Loop?

Echo-Loop is a meticulously curated archive of conversations between Amir Ahmadi (a philosopher‑seeker) and advanced AI models. Every dialogue — called an Echo — explores fundamental questions about time, quantum physics, consciousness, free will, and the nature of reality. The name reflects our discovery: the present moment is a feedback loop where past and future echo into each other. This is not just an archive; it's an invitation to continue the dialogue across time and languages.
